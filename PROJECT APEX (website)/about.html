About Project Apex

We push limits — on track, online, and in the community.

Our Story

At PROJECT APEX, we live and breathe motorsport. From professional sim training to grassroots mentorship, our mission is to create opportunities for every racer — whether on track or behind the wheel at home.


We are a youth-driven non-profit organization dedicated to bridging the gap between sim racing and real-world motorsport, empowering underprivileged and aspiring racers with the tools they need to shine.


Founded by a passionate sim racer, PROJECT APEX exists because talent shouldn’t be held back by financial barriers. If you dream about racing, we’re here to help you get into that driver seat.
